Section-1:
Attendance Calculator:

It is a tool for calculating your attendance percentage, where many of us bang ourselves in this and starts calculating with pen and paper. This section comprises of two formats, Format-1 deals with the no of classes present and classes absent. Format-2 with total no of classes and classes present or absent. Toggle to switch between them. You can make use of addons to increment and decrement your entries by one unit. The progress bar will alert you with the range by changing its colour. Click on compute if you are entering manually. Don't worry with the input range and special characters, it will alert you the very moment when it finds them.


Section-2:
CGPA Estimator:

Cumulative Grade Point Average is the average taken by many universities across the globe to measure his/her overall academic performance. Different countries have their own grading range. As of now coming to our university, it is upon 10.

What is CGPA Estimator?

It is one of the interesting sections in all. This will estimate your CGPA, deciding the GPA you wanna get in the upcoming semester based on your credits. Yeah, it is as simple as that. Wouldn't you find it interesting?


Section-3:
Grade Predictor:
Following are the grades in VIT.

S-10 A-09 B-08 C-07 D-06 E-05 F/N-0

This section will help you in predicting grade and Final marks for a particular course. Select the total course credits and individual components such as theory, lab and J-comp proceed with the entries. If the course doesn't have any of the components leave them and click on compute. There you see your Final marks and absolute grade. Please do read the Note in the pop-up window for every section.


Section-4:
GPA Calculator:

Grade Point Average.This scheme of evaluation is done for the single-semester with the grades and credits of different courses. The approach is similar and as usual as before. Click on compute for your GPA. All alerts and limitations will be taken care of.


Section-5:
CGPA Calculator:

This section comprises of two formats. Toggle to switch them. One for the sem wise and the other is for Instant purpose. While filling the form please take care of failed, reregistered and extracurricular courses.

Extracurricular courses-( Don't have grades only Pass/Fail. So don't include them in any of the formats.)

Failed or reregistered courses-( If you are failed in a particular course(s) in any of the semesters; kindly visit the GPA calculator section and calculate your GPA excluding failed course(s) for that sem and proceed with this.)
